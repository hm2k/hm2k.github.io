Suprnova.org - CLOSED FOREVER!
After it was mentioned last week that the MPAA were going after BitTorrent websites, and also mention of it in the news in the UK it seems that http://www.suprnova.org/ has closed.

Like almost all the BitTorrent sites at the moment, it has finally closed due to pressure from governments.

However for now it seems that http://www.thepiratebay.org/ are avoiding UK and US laws as they are located in Sweden, but its only a matter of time...

You may also find that http://www.lokitorrent.com/ is up, however it seems they are facing legal issues and require its users to donate.

Maybe once again, warez will go underground and return to IRC channels.

If you have an IRC client, you can join #bt, #bt-gm and #tvtorrents on EFnet.
