Lindsay Lohan caused a massive dDoS war
It all began when Eric Bauman of Ebaums world decided to steal content from ytmnd.com.

This isn't the first time Eric has stolent content, no. Infact there's an entire website, flash movie and song dedicated to that fact, which is eBaumsworldsucks.com.

How does this related to Lindsay Lohan you may ask, well if you visit the (now changed) Lindsay Lohan page on ytmnd.com you will see an animated gif of Lindsay Lohan.

A few hours later it seems this same animated gif ended up on eBaumsworld, with his trusty watermark on it. http://ebaumsworld.com/lohanfacial.html

It seems that Eric picked the wrong site to mess with. YTMND and their community are pretty annoyed.

Within a couple of hours a flame war grew into a fully fledged cyber war. Practically the entire Ytmnd community, combined with a sizeable chunk of Somethingawful.com and Newsgrounds descended upon the Ebaumsworld livechat, forums and techsupport. Chaos ensued, with beastly ping floods, DoS and chunks of code apparently uploaded onto sections of Ebaumsworld. Predictably, it also aroused the interest of numerous script kiddies, who are currently running a competition to see who can wipe out Ebaums world completely.

But this wasn't the only thing to come from all of this, a number of sites were put up at ytmnd.com:
