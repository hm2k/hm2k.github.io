What is CJB?
CJB is the initials of the CJB Management, Inc founder. CJB.NET offers free web hosting, free domains, free forums, and free blogs. CJB.NET is a registered trademark of CJB Management, Inc.

Using CJB.NET I have created a script that will lookup your IP address, and using the CJB.NET Dynip page it will update your account with your ip address automatically.

This script allows you using mIRC sockets to connect to CJB.NET and change your IP or URL assigned to your account, and you can even make it update to your own IP automaticly when you connect to IRC.

Please sign up for a CJB.NET account before loading the script.
