Xmas iShopping
I went for a trek to Manchester yesterday, to pick up some final bits and peices for Christmas. I ended up at the Trafford Center - Possibily the biggest shopping center in the UK.

It was a bit of a rat race, everyone for themselves, leaping for that bargin they have been hunting down.

Thats all very well, however I couldn't believe it when on my travels around Mount Trafford, when I saw an Apple Shop, and we're not talking granny smiths here, i'm talking about a shop dedicated to Apple and their products.

It got me thinking, how can this exist, how can this be, why can this be so popular, when lets face it Mac OS has sucked since the dawn of time, even more than Windows. Its a fashion statement, nothing more.

To top off my findings, I have the very latest in Apple products... thats right its the iProduct...
