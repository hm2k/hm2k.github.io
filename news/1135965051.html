WGA work around
A friend told me about this WGA work around the other day. Kinda handy...

1. Open Internet Explorer
2. Goto Tools, Manage Addons
3. Select Addons that have been used by IE
4. Highlight Windows Genuwine Advantage
5. Click the radio button "disable" and hit OK
6. Restart Internet Explorer

I tested it, and it worked! Enjoy.
