Microsoft AntiSpyware becomes Windows Defender
This is the final name for the long awaited (free) Microsoft solution to Spyware and other forms of Malware.

Hopefully now, it won't be long before the software comes out of beta and into final, since they now have the final name for the product.

And its a good one, its positive and its cool. I actually quite like this product as it was not originally created by Microsoft, it was created by a company called GIANT, which makes it easy to realise that it is actually a good peice of software.

It seems to me, all these new products and names for products are lining up for one thing, that is Windows Vista or Windows Live. Its only a matter of time.
