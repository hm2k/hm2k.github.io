BT Privacy at Home
For all those in the UK that have not signed up to this service, I would recommend doing so now for FREE!

Sign up to the BT Privacy at Home Service here or call 0800 121 8000.

This service includes Caller Display for free, which means you can see what number is calling you on your phone's display (providing its a fairly new one).

You can also register with the DMA to no longer receive annoying marketing phone calls.
