Removing or reporting an undetected virus
Recently i've had people report that they have received a file that is disguised as something else. Yes a Trojan.

Trojans have been around for years, but what do you do if you receive one that is undetected by your virus checker, well this is what I decided to look into...

To remove and report this virus you can do the following:
1, Make sure all you have a firewall running. If you run Windows XP with Service Pack 2, you should be fine. Otherwise download a copy of Kerio Personal Firewall v2.
2, Make sure you are up-to-date. Most importantly that your Windows Updates are complete, and secondly that your AntiVirus software is up-to-date.
3, Use another Virus checker to see if it can detect it. Trendmicro offer a free online based virus checker called housecall.
4, Remove any viruses. You can do this by using HiJackThis.
5, Report the virus. Its difficult to find places that will allow you to send viruses for analysis. But you can email new viruses to "newvirus@kaspersky.com" and kaspersky will get back to you.
