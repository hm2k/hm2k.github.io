Remembrance Day Poppy Appeal
Aka Armistice Day

Yes, its that time of year again, and yes I bought my poppy, I have bought one almost every year of my life as it was enforced at school/college, then a lovely old dear of a lady brings them round personally to my house.



If you missed your one minutes silents at 11am today, try again at 11am on Sunday.

However I was thinking, where does this money go for this appear, isn't there only like one guy left from the World War I now, since all the others died off?

Even so, they will all be in our memories we owe it to them that our country isn't a German one (Yes I am aware that the Royal Family is German, but never mind...)
