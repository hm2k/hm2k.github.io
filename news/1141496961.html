Windows Live Messenger 8.0 Beta
People have been asking me what the new messenger live is like.

Well its better than the last beta, thats for sure.

On the previous beta, the names/email addresses of users were getting mixed up, and the user list was really badly designed.

Here is a screenshot:


Its far better than before, but there doesn't seem to be that many differences between this version and MSN Messenger 7.5, apart from the interface is a bit different, and it uses a new protocol.

This is all part of the new windows live platform Microsoft are rolling out. All there ideas are looking fairly cool, I believe its a step in the right direction for Microsoft.
