BitTorrents
I'm rather fed up of people asking me about BitTorrents, and where to find them etc, so I thought i'd let you all know with a few links:

http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/BitTorrent - A wiki page about BitTorrents, always updated.
http://pingpong-abc.sourceforge.net/download.php - Download a ittorrent client from here, I recommend this one.
http://azureus.sourceforge.net/ - Another client I have used recently, its actually quite good, although it is written in Java and can be slow...
http://www.lokitorrent.com/ - A popular indexer...
http://www.thepiratebay.org/ - Another popular indexer...
http://www.torrentreactor.net/ - A very searchable and active indexer...
http://www.torrentspy.com/ Yet another...
